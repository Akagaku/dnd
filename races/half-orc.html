Half-Orc

Something something.

Subrace

There are two subraces of half-orc, one for each species of orc in the isles: Ezakh and Yacal. Choose one of the two subraces before creating your character. You gain all of the traits listed under the Aasimar Traits section, as well as those listed under your chosen subrace.

Half-Orc Traits

  • Ability Score Increase. Your Strength score increases by 2.
  • Age. Half-orcs reach maturity around the age of 14, and rarely live longer than 60 years.
  • Speed. Your base walking speed is 30 feet.
  • Menacing. You are proficient in the Intimidation skill.

Ezakh

The Ezakhs are a barbaric race of orc from the far-flung north. Daunting in their size and animalistic appearance, they have been feared for centuries by northern humans and dwarves alike. Their strength, durability, and ferocity make them formidable opponents, and their love of treasure and violence make them insatiable killers. Like their orcish sires, Ezakh half-orcs make excellent shock troopers, can effortlessly chase down fleeing opponents, and can fight on even in the face of death. They are hulking in size, and have cold, gray skin.

  • Size. Ezakh half-orcs tower above humans, standing between 6 and 7 feet tall.
  • Relentless Endurance. When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hit point instead. You must finish a long rest to use this feature again.
  • Savage Attacks. When you score a critical hit with a melee weapon attack, you roll one extra damage die.r
  • Charger. As a bonus action, you can move up to 30 feet in a straight line toward a target that you can see. Afterward, you must take the Attack action and make a melee weapon attack against the target.
  • Languages. You can speak, read, and write Tenian and Ezakh.

Yacal

The Yacal tribe is a large, slightly more civilized group of orcs in the southen jungles of Selvana. Even during the age of dragons, they were ancient enemies of early humans. They are naturally skilled and stealthy hunters, and they stalk the dense forests of the southern isle with an unmatched ruthless efficiency. Their half-human children share their skill and penchant for brutality, and are natural-born hunters. They are smaller and slighter than the northern Ezakh, and other than their green skin, are more human in appearance.

  • Size. Yacal half-orcs range from 5 and a half feet to 6 and a half feet tall.
  • Jungle Fighter. You gain proficiency in two skills from the following list: Animal Handling, Athletics, Nature, Stealth, Survival.
  • Darkvision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern colors in darkness, only shades of gray.
  • Guerilla Tactics. You have advantage on attacks made against targets who have not yet acted in combat.
  • Languages. You can speak, read, and write Tenian and Yacaltl.
