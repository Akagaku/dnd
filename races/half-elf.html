Half-Elf Traits

  • Ability Score Increase. One ability score of your choice increases by 2.
  • Skill Versatility. You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice.

Sothonian

  • Heirs of Evrys. You have proficiency with shields and one weapon from the following list: shortsword, shortbow, spear.
  • Sons of the Dragonslayers. You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened.
  • Languages. You can speak, read, and write Tenian, Sothonian, and one other language of your choice.

Alkarian

  • Dim Light Vision. You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light.
  • Languages. You can speak, read, and write Tenian, Alkarian, and one other language of your choice.
