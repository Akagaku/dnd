The Wounded Isles

"The Wounded Isles" is the coloquialism used by locals to describe the landmasses of Hircron and Minyron, named for an ancient legend in which the land was split in the aftermath of the battle between Bahamut and Tiamat, which ultimately resulted in the deaths of both and the eventual downfall of dragon civilziation.

Hircron

The northern isle, and the larger of the two, Hircron is the home of the former Liran Empire and her daughter states: the Kingdom of Lira, Ashvogel, Free Rock, and Sothonia. Its diverse landscape consists of forests, mountains, hills, plains, marshlands, and tundras.

Minyron

The southern isle, and the smaller of the two, Minyron is the home of the nations of Selvana and Alkaria. Its hot, tropical climate houses the Vermara jungle, and the vast desert expanses of al-Qara min Nar, the Sea of Fire.
